How to get there

4km from the pretty royal city of Loches
less than 40 km from Tours
270 km from Paris

  

By Car

From Paris:

Follow A10 to Autrèche.
Take exit 18 from A10
Take D31, D943 and D31 to destination

from South:

Take A75 and A71.
Take exit 11 from A85/E604 to D31
Take D31, D943 and D31 to destination
